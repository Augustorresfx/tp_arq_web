
REGISTER
curl.exe -X POST "http://localhost:4001/auth/register" `
>> -H "Content-Type: application/json" `
>> -d '{\"email\":\"test@test.com\",\"password\":\"123456\", \"role\": \"admin\"}'

LOGIN
curl.exe -X POST "http://localhost:4001/auth/login" `
>> -H "Content-Type: application/json" `
>> -d '{\"email\":\"test@test.com\",\"password\":\"123456\"}'

- devuelve el accessToken y refreshToken
